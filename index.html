MacBooks vs. Windows laptops: How do you choose?

Mac has a simpler design and interface

Although Windows is the most widely used operating system in the world, there are many reasons why you might choose a Mac. Here are some advantages and disadvantages of Mac compared to Windows:

Advantages of Mac:

The Top view of the Mac Laptop. Sleek, and ready to be used!

  • Mac is known for its high quality and durability. Apple uses high quality materials to make its products and also provides excellent customer service.
  • Mac has a simpler design and interface, which makes it easier to use for many users.
  • Mac is less vulnerable to viruses and malware, which increases security.

Disadvantages of Mac:

  1. Mac is usually more expensive than Windows computers.
  2. There is less software and games for Mac, because Windows is the most used operating system.
  3. Closed system: MacBooks have a closed system and there are fewer customization options than with a Windows laptop or PC.
